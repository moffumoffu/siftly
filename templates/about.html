How Siftly works

A simple AI pipeline that reads the news so you don't have to.

01

Fetch from Hacker News

Siftly pulls a random selection of 20 posts from the top 100 stories on Hacker News using their public Firebase API. No scraping, no login required.

02

Analyze with AI

Each post title is sent to Groq's API running Llama 3.3 70B. The model returns a verdict (Useful, Noise, or Spam), a quality score out of 10, and a one-sentence summary.

03

Filter and sort

Results are displayed as cards with color-coded verdicts. You can filter by category or sort by upvotes and quality score to find what matters most.

04

Refresh anytime

Hit refresh to pull a fresh random batch. Each load is a different slice of the top 100, so you're never seeing the exact same feed twice.

Tech stack
